About Bernardo

∎

Bernardo Teixeira is a senior pursuing a bachelor in business with a finance concentration and a computer science minor. Bernardo just finished his thrid coop with Credit Suisse HOLT in Chicago. For his second co-op, Bernardo worked with HSBC Global Asset Management (AMG) in Hong Kong, where he works for the Insurance Coverage team developing research material for institutional clients. Together with Bowen Li, Connor Flood and some other SVF members Bernardo has been helping SVF with an on going restructure and revamp as of June 2013. Since then Bernardo was Senior Analyst of the Energy and Basic Materials team (2013) and Portfolio Manager (2014). During his term as Portfolio manager his key goal was to improve the teams financial and technical knowledge. As senior analyst he plans on continuing the process of developing the group skills by sharing much of the knowledge he gained working for HSBC and HOLT.
